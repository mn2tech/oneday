Day Schedule

An afternoon full of joy, tradition, and love 💛

12:00 PM
🚪Guests Arrive & Welcome
Arrive and be welcomed with flower garlands. Enjoy traditional refreshments and mingling with family and friends.
12:30 PM
🙏Opening Ceremony & Prayers
Traditional prayers and blessings led by the elders of the family to mark the auspicious beginning of the Nalugu ceremony.
1:00 PM
🎶Traditional Music Begins
Live folk music and songs fill the venue as preparations begin. Feel free to clap and sing along!
1:30 PM
💛Turmeric Paste Ceremony
The heartbeat of the celebration! Family and close friends lovingly apply turmeric paste to the bride, showering her with blessings and well-wishes.
2:30 PM
💃Dance & Celebration
Joyful group dances and performances by family members. Everyone is invited to join the dance floor!
3:30 PM
🍛Festive Traditional Feast
A grand spread of traditional dishes served on banana leaves — biryanis, curries, sweets, and more. A true feast to celebrate Nadia!
5:00 PM
🌅Farewell & Blessings
Closing blessings for the bride. Guests depart with sweet treats and beautiful memories from this magical day.
👗 Dress Code
Bright Traditional Attire
Yellows, Oranges, and Pinks are encouraged! 🌼
Wear your most vibrant traditional outfit and help make Nadia's day even more colorful!
